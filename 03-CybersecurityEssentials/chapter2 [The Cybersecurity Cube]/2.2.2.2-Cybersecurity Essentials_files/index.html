Critical Level
Healthcare and emergency services:
  1. All data is validated and tested
  2. Data is verified to provide trustworthiness
  3. Examples include healthcare and financial records
High Level
Ecommerce and analytics:
  1. All data is validated
  2. Data is checked to provide trustworthiness
  3. Examples include organizations' databases
Mid Level
Online sales and search engines:
  1. Little verification is performed
  2. Data not completely trustworthy
  3. Data is collected with publicly posted forms
Low Level
Blogs and personal posting sites:
  1. Data may not be verified
  2. Low level of trust in content
  3. Examples include public opinion and open contribution
