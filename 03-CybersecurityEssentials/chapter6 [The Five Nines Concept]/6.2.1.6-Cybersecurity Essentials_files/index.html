Accept
Periodically re-assess accepted risks in ongoing processes as a normal feature of business operations and modify mitigation measures.
Reduce
Design a new business process with adequate built-in risk control and containment measures from the start.
Avoid
Avoid risks altogether would include measures such as physically disconnecting from the Internet.
Transfer
Transfer risks to an external agency (a service level agreement or insurance company).
